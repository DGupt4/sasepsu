SASE Commonwealth Connect

What is the Commonwealth Connect?


The SASE Commonwealth Connect is a new initiative by SASE to reach out to Commonwealth campus students and assist them in the process of moving up to the University Park campus. Through this initiative members of SASE will provide weekly office hours where Commonwealth campus students can reach out and ask us any questions on the process of moving up to the University Park campus or about SASE and our mission.
